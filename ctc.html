Yahaya Buhari Ali

Yahaya Buhari Ali is a TED Speaker,  Serial Entrepreneur, Digital marketing expert (Google certified),  Digital PR strategist, Business consultant, Nation builder & CEO YBI GROUP.



Yahaya Buhari obtained his Bsc. Biochemistry from Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto 2015, he then furthered his education by enrolling in a PG program at kaduna state university and bagged a diploma in entrepreneurship in 2017, he has so many certificates and awards on entrepreneurship and personal development.



He is the Founder and CEO of YB GROUP 2015 till date.


YBI GROUP is one of the biggest startup conglomerate in Nigeria, it comprises of YB INNOVATIONS (Branding, printing and packaging), YBI MULTIMEDIA (Digital media), YB EXPRESS (logistics) and YB CONSULTS (consultancy). It is founded and managed by Yahaya Buhari Ali with a team of about 65 staff, it is located in Kaduna State Nigeria. 

Yahaya Buhari Ali is  Happily Married.

Umar Kabir Dan Anini

Umar Kabir Dan Anini is a TED Speaker, Computer Scientist from Usmanu Danfodiyo University Sokoto on a mission to train as many people as possible in terms of digital skills especially in hausa community to better their lives, businesses and also be safe online and has so far trained over 30,000 Nigerian Youth on various Digital Skills. 

He has acquired many digital certifications in Nigeria and abroad including digital marketing from Google and Digital Literacy from Microsoft.
He has been a Google IT Trainer since 2016.
 
Over the years he has been organizing physical and virtual Trainings: helping people, businesses, organizations and government agencies to adapt ICT tools for better.

He's the founder of Dan Anini Digital Solutions where they provide simple and innovative solutions to digital problems. 

In 2022 he founded Ciabinci.com which is the first Hausa based easy to use Food delivery System in Sokoto.

He is the Head of Unit Software & System Solutions at Sokoto State ICT Directorate. 


He is the IT Coordinator for the Center for Entrepreneurship Development Usmanu Danfodiyo University Sokoto,

He is the Professional IT Assistant to Executive Chairman Sokoto State Media Corporation.

He also Assistant Technical Lead at Startup Sokoto. 

In 2020 he received an award as the "Tech young Achiever of the year" 

Umar Kabir Dan Anini is 26 years old and happily married.
